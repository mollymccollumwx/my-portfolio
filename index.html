About Me

Molly is a full-time student at Georgia Tech's Coding Bootcamp through the university's Professional Eduation program. She graduated with her bachelor of science in Atmospheric Sciences and Geography from the University of Georgia. She worked as a TV meteorologist for six years in Wichita, Kansas, Tulsa, Oklahoma, and most recently in her hometown of Atlanta, Georgia. Molly believes that digital is the future and hopes her newly honed web-developing skills will create new opportunites for her both in the tech and meteorology industries. In her classes, she is getting hands-on experience with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, React.js, Express.js, Node.js, MongoDB, MySQL, Git and more. Check back periodically to find new projects in her portfolio.

In her free time, Molly spends a lot of time with her two dogs, Charlie and Elsie. She also likes to hike, run, and spend as much time outdoors as possible.
